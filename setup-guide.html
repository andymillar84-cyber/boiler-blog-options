Your New Blog:
3 Small Jobs to Finish

About 15 minutes in total

Hi Graeme,

Good news. Your blog is working. It writes articles about boilers and posts them by itself.

There are 3 small jobs left. Only you can do them, because they need your logins.

Each job has its own page. Click the green button to start a job. You can do them one at a time — there's no rush.

Before you start, have these ready:

  • Your bank card
  • Your Webflow login
  • Your Names.co.uk login (where your web address is kept)
  • Your Google login
JOB 1

Set up automatic posting

⏱ About 5 minutes · needs your bank card

Upgrade the blog tool to the "Pro" plan, so it posts new articles all by itself.

Why it matters: without this, a person has to press the post button for every article. With it, the blog runs with no thinking at all. (This one is optional — the page explains.)
Start Job 1 →
JOB 2

Put the blog on your web address

⏱ About 5 minutes, plus a short wait · needs your Webflow and Names.co.uk logins

Move the blog onto blog.commercialboilersolutions.co.uk — your own web address.

Why it matters: right now the blog sits on a free address that has nothing to do with your business. On your own address, every article builds up your company's name on Google. It also looks more professional to customers.
Start Job 2 →
JOB 3

Let Google find your blog

⏱ About 3 minutes · needs your Google login · do this AFTER Job 2

Connect the blog to Google, so Google reads each new article.

Why it matters: Google does not know a new article exists until it's told. Without this, the articles sit unseen — and customers never find them.
