Set Up Automatic Posting

Takes about 5 minutes

← Back to all jobs

Right now the blog is on the basic plan. It costs $49 US a month (about £38). On this plan, the blog still writes articles — but a person has to press the post button for each one.

The "Pro" plan makes it fully automatic. The blog writes and posts new articles all by itself. No thinking, no buttons, nothing to remember.

To upgrade, these are the steps:

  1. Go to app.trysight.ai and log in.
  2. Find the Billing page. It is in the settings menu.
  3. Add your bank card.
  4. Pick the plan called Pro.
Good to know: Pro costs $129 US a month. That is about £100.

That's it. Andrew will turn on the autopilot after this is done.

This one is your choice: the Pro plan is only needed for automatic posting. If you would rather press the post button yourself for each article, you can skip this job and stay on the cheaper plan.
